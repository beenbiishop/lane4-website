Our Services

Lane 4 provides strategic fundraising counsel, campaign planning, executive coaching, and structured support designed to help mission-driven organizations grow with greater clarity and confidence.

Annual Fund Design and Execution

Build structure, momentum, and discipline into your fundraising year.

We partner with organizations to design and execute comprehensive annual fundraising programs that bring clarity, structure, and momentum to the development calendar.

Through a 12 month partnership, Lane 4 helps you create a master fundraising plan that ensures every event is intentional, builds on what comes before and after, and reflects your organization values and mission. We work alongside your team to reimagine fundraising events, donor recognition, database systems, appeal cycles, major gifts, grants, sponsorships, and donor touchpoints, strengthening your fundraising culture and aligning everyone around shared goals. We also bring expertise in sponsorship mapping and packaging, transforming line items in your budget into compelling opportunities for donor support.

We use proven tracking tools that keep everyone on the same page, ensuring accountability and real time visibility across your development efforts. Lane 4 specializes in working with organizations with operating budgets between $500,000 and $2 million that are at a tipping point of growth, ready to implement new systems for long term sustainability.

Feasibility Studies

Create clarity before the ask.

A well executed feasibility study is powerful work. When done right, it draws stakeholders closer to your mission and lays the groundwork for transformational support without making the ask.

Our approach quickly integrates us into your leadership team, equipping you with campaign projections, messaging, and leadership alignment to make clear, confident decisions about your future.

Capital Campaigns and Special Initiatives

Align leadership and strategy around transformational growth.

We partner with organizations ready to make transformative change through major funding initiatives. This may include capital or endowment campaigns, planned and legacy giving programs, anniversary milestones, or programmatic initiatives.

By developing a strategic campaign plan, aligning leadership, coaching solicitation teams, conducting prospect research, and partnering with you on key asks, we craft strategies that help you reach your goals and take the next big step forward.

Executive Fundraising Coaching

Help leaders become stronger, more confident fundraisers.

Leadership teams often have executive coaches, but rarely fundraising coaches. Lane 4 provides specialized coaching partnerships for chief executives and executive directors to help them excel in solicitation and closing gifts.

This can include positioning your development director for success, inspiring board engagement, and creating compelling sponsorship opportunities that deepen relationships. We also coach directors of development, advise on building a strong fundraising culture, and share best practices from our experience in fundraising and executive leadership.

Retreats and Facilitation

Bring teams together around clear priorities and next steps.

Well planned retreats and committee meetings give everyone a voice and align your team around shared priorities. Lane 4 facilitates group strategy sessions that spark fresh thinking, clarify priorities, highlight strengths, and leave participants energized and united.

Whether you need a planning session to align board and administrative priorities or a focused retreat on fundraising skills, we help design sessions that move your organization toward its goals.

Projects We Define Together

Tailored solutions for challenges that do not fit a standard mold.

Our core services provide a strong foundation, but we also thrive on creative approaches and unconventional strategies. If your organization faces a unique challenge, we are ready to explore it with you and build something impactful together.
